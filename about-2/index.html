Singhal Group of Industries

The companies are operating their integrated steel plants in Raigarh, Chhattisgarh. It took us 22 years to establish a solid foundation for growth and success, and we continue to build upon it since starting commercial production of our first plant.

The Group achieved success by having a well-researched strategy that prioritizes size, high-quality products, and excellent customer service.

Mission

Our mission is to be a leading iron and steel manufacturer, committed to providing high quality products and services that exceed our customers’ expectations while minimizing the environmental impact. 

Vision

To become an innovative, unique, and competitive steel and iron company that provides the best quality of products and unmatched customer service. 

Founder's Message

The iron and steel industry has been the backbone of the industrial development of India,  which also has contributed to transforming the economy of the nation. In the last decade, India has secured a top place among the largest producer of sponge iron and raw steel that is expected to expand in the coming years. Hence, keeping the upcoming demands of steel and iron products in mind and bridging the gap between manufacture and supply, Singhal Group was established in 2000. 

With a vision to fulfil the demands of the automotive and construction industry, we set up Integrated Steel Plants (ISP) in a phased manner comprising a Sponge Iron Plant, Power Plant, Induction Furnace, Rolling Mill and Ferro Alloys Plant. Our daily efforts are directed towards providing satisfactory services while achieving milestones in leadership, excellence, competitive prices, and quality.   

From commencement, we’ve seen immense organic growth as we began from a single product and expanded gradually into an integrated steel plant. Looking ahead, we aim to become a profitable, unique, reliable and competitive steel and iron company that provides exclusive business solutions by manufacturing goods. 

We also aim to invest in steel industries and other potential areas to contribute to the industrial as well as economic growth as a leading firm. Furthermore, we strive for social responsibility through community development by leveraging our core competencies, resources and knowledge base. 

We would be happy if you choose to be a part of our journey. 

Regards,

Parmeshwar Das Agarwal 

Manufacturing facilities

Manufacturing facilities

Our company has its manufacturing facilities in the Taraimal area of Raigarh (C.G.), spanning over an area of more than 400 acres. Our plants are conveniently located alongside the Raigarh-Ambikapur State Highway, approximately 22 kilometers away from Raigarh Railway Station. Additionally, the Salasar Steel and Power unit is situated along the same highway in the Gerwani area, around 20 kilometers from the Raigarh Railway Station.

Currently, a private railway siding is under construction at Kotarlia Railway Station, approximately 35 kilometers from our plant. This siding is located on the Bilaspur-Jharsuguda route of the South-eastern Central Railway, providing us with an efficient transportation option.

As an environmentally conscious organization, we have taken measures to convert a significant portion of our total area into green belts by conducting various plantation drives over the years. We have also utilized the Miyawaki forestation technique to develop dense forests on our land.

Milestones / Timeline
